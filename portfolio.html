Philosophy →

Bridging digital and physical worlds through thoughtful, human-centered design

My approach to design is rooted in the belief that the best solutions emerge from the intersection of traditional craftsmanship and cutting-edge technology. Whether I'm sketching initial concepts or prototyping in the makerspace, I'm driven by a curiosity about how things work and how they can work better.

Drawing has been my constant companion throughout this journey, informing everything from product ideation to digital interface design. I find that hand-drawn elements bring a human quality to even the most technologically advanced projects.

As I approach graduation, I'm excited to apply my creative technology background to solve meaningful problems and create experiences that resonate with people on both functional and emotional levels.
